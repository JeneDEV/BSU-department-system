DEPARTMENT:

College of Engineering Technology (CET)

BatStateU-TNEU

The College of Industrial Technology (CIT) is one of the premier departments in Batangas State University-JPLPC Malvar. The college offers Bachelor of Industrial Technology (BIT) since 2001. It delivers Dual Training System (DTS) which provides Vocational, Technical and Technician training programs. The DTS is a special type of vocational training that combines two places of learning: the school and the factory. It is called the Dual System since learning takes place both in the classroom and in the company.
